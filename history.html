1944

1998- Dr. Gregory W. Kallos and Harlan Weingart purchased the old Continental Bank Building at 122 No. 11th Street amid rumors that It was going to be condemned. The 104-year-old building housed only two tenants at the time and the upper floors had not been occupied for several years.

=======

2020

After adding new windows, installing a new roof, and remodeling the tenant spaced on the first floor, demolition began on the Interior of the upper levels. Running Into false walls, bank vaults surrounded by several feet of concrete and other obstacles made the demolition process longer than expected. The building was eventually gutted to its exterior walls. New wiring, plumbing, heating, and cooling systems were added, a state-of-the-art security system was Installed and plans were drawn up for the four upper floors.
