About Us

Continental Commons is home The Happy Raven bar and The Dish restaurant, but we also offer office spaces, studio apartments, and luxury condominiums.

In 2001 Continental Commons received the Residential Development Award from the Downtown Lincoln Association.

Downtown has changed from its earlier years and has evolved into a vibrant center of businesses of all types, both large and small, government and education, tourism, arts, non-profit organizations, visitors, and residents. Downtown is a great place to live or start your business.
